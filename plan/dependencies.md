- OAuth provider integration, JWT claim design, key rotation
- SSO support for enterprise tenants

## Security Team

- Infrastructure provisioning, autoscaling, CI/CD
- Observability stack and on-call processes

## Product & Design
- Dashboard UX, metrics definitions, role permissions
- Tenant onboarding flows

## Legal & Compliance
- SOC 2 control alignment, evidence repository
- Data retention and privacy policy review
